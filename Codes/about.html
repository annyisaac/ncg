Our Story

Founded in 2019, NCGR LTD began its journey with a focus on Ross 308 parent stock, renowned for its efficient meat production. Over the years, NCGR LTD has become a trusted supplier of day-old broiler chicks, serving a wide network of poultry farmers.

2019

NCGR LTD was founded with a focus on Ross 308 parent stock management and day-old broiler chick supply. The company established itself as a reliable partner for poultry farmers seeking high-quality genetics for efficient meat production.

2024

A significant milestone was achieved when NCGR LTD obtained official authorization to rear Tetra Super Harco, a dual-purpose breed admired for its adaptability to free-range conditions, robust egg-laying performance, and excellent meat quality.

This strategic expansion positions NCGR LTD as one of providers of both broiler and dual-purpose genetics in the region, meeting diverse poultry farming needs with professionalism and precision.
